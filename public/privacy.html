Keyman Assistant Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

Keyman Assistant helps keymen create volunteer shift rotations, save rotation details to an in-app calendar, track checklist progress, and keep discussion or emergency-plan information close at hand.

Information We Collect

The app uses an email address and password to create and access an account. Passwords are stored as salted hashes. The app can also store volunteer names and phone numbers, shift dates, rotation schedules, checklist progress, and discussion notes that you enter or confirm after an image scan.

Roster Image Recognition

Roster screenshots and images are selected by you and processed on your device to recognize names and phone numbers. Keyman Assistant does not upload or retain the source image. You review recognized information before adding it to a shift.

How We Use Information

Information is used to sign you in, keep your account secure, support shift-planning features, and prepare messages that you choose whether to send. We do not sell personal information. If you create a group message, recipients may see one another’s phone numbers; the app displays a warning first.

Storage

Account information and confirmed shift-planning data, including saved volunteer contact information, are stored on the Keyman Assistant backend and cached on the device so the app can continue to display recent information.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from the Profile page in the app. Deleting your account removes the account and saved shift planning data from the backend and clears app data from the device.

Children

Keyman Assistant is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The updated date above will reflect the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the support information listed on the App Store page.
