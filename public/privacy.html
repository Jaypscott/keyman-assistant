Keyman Assistant Privacy Policy

Last updated May 19, 2026

Keyman Assistant helps keymen create volunteer shift rotations, save rotation details to an in-app calendar, track checklist progress, and keep discussion or emergency-plan information close at hand.

Information We Collect

The app uses an email address and password to create and access an account. Passwords are stored as salted hashes. The app can also store volunteer names, shift dates, rotation schedules, checklist progress, and discussion notes that you enter.

How We Use Information

Information is used to sign you in, keep your account secure, and support shift-planning features inside the app. We do not sell personal information.

Storage

Account information is stored on the Keyman Assistant backend. Shift planning data is stored locally on the device unless a future version adds cloud sync.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from the Profile page in the app. Deleting your account removes the account from the backend and clears app data from the device.

Children

Keyman Assistant is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The updated date above will reflect the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the support information listed on the App Store page.
